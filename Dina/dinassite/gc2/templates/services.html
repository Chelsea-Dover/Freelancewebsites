Glassman Coaching and Consulting (GC2) Service Offerings and Glassman Background

  • Coaching Offering:

    Deliver individual coaching services through six-month engagements (with option to renew) to the following potential clients:

    • Professionals transitioning into new role or work arrangement (e.g. lateral hires)
    • Expecting and/or newly returning parents
    • Professionals navigating/managing alternative schedules
    • Newly promoted professionals
    • Highest performers approaching promotion
    • Departing/transitioning professionals (e.g. retirement, assumption of new role within company, managed transition, office move)
    • Diverse professionals
    • Committee leaders and group/office chairs taking on new administrative responsibilities

  • Proposed Structure for Coaching Engagements

    • 6-month engagements include bi-monthly individual meetings, program design, intake, firm/company intake, ongoing support/correspondence
    • Retainer arrangement option: in-office presence on a half-day or full day basis two times per month; include breakfast or lunch meeting with management representative in addition to open coaching hours
    • Group coaching facilitations (including intake and consult with sponsor, debrief)
