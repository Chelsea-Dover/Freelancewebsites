About Dina

Dina's Story

With nearly 20 years of corporate experience, I am passionate about helping professionals awaken to what life success looks like for them, and guiding them towards it. My own career transitions have included moving cross-country to join Perkins Coie as a lateral labor and employment associate, being the first professional in the Portland office to transition to a reduced hours schedule, leaving the practice of law to become the first Director of Professional Development in the Portland market, and then transitioning to the position I created as an internal career coach. For almost two decades, I have spoken widely on a host of professional development and diversity topics.

Dina’s Background and Experience

  • Recipient of Perkins Coie’s inaugural Firmwide Diversity Champion Award
  • JD from NYU Law School; BA from Colgate University, phi beta kappa, magna cum laude
  • MBSR (Mindfulness Based Stress Reduction) program graduate (OHSU)
  • 19 years inside Big Law (AmLaw 50 firms); DLA Piper and Perkins Coie
  • 7 years litigation practice experience at DLA and Perkins Coie; first lawyer to work a reduced schedule in the Portland office of Perkins Coie
  • 10+ years of Big Firm Attorney Development experience, including 5 years as Director of Attorney Development at Perkins Coie, a role I co-created, which remains the most senior attorney development role in the firm
  • 4 years as internal Attorney Career Coach, including experience coaching 100+ individual lawyers, across all seniority levels, 14 geographic locations and 8 practice groups (developed the job description for the role, one of the first designated internal law firm coaching roles in the country)
  • Co-founder of Internal Coaching Peer Group, including more than a dozen coaching professionals inside Big Law
  • Longstanding member of Professional Development Consortium (PDC)
  • Individual coaching engagements with more than 120 professionals across a range of seniority levels and practice areas
